1. Preface from the new editors

With a few days delayed the new ESSA newsletter issue #218 is spread from the new editors. It is a pleasure for us to relieve Nick Gotts and Shah Jamal Alam who had been responsible for the ESSA-Newsletter for years, spend much time in compiling all the information and did a very good job.

With us beeing new editors also come along some new ideas regarding organisation and layout of the content. Most of you may have noticed that there is a large amount of information for the ESSA members. However, not every peace of information is of interest to everyone. That is why we decided to tag contents according to SIGs (and further topics). However, not all items were tagged in this issue, and due to server configuration the color coding doesn't work so far. Furthermore, items are ordered by - hopefully - meaningful criteria. See notes below the headers for details. Furthermore, we created google forms that allow everyone to enter in information about conferences, publications, job offers, and news, which helps us saving a lot of effort and ensures that the right information is at the right place. Do not forget to leave a name and email address in the "Other" field when you submit content:

  • Conferences: Call for papers
  • Conferences: Participation
  • Job Offers
  • Publications
  • News

Finally we hope that these changes meet your needs, and we are looking forward to any feedback in order to further improve the newsletter. Now, enjoy gathering all the information.

Richard and Sascha
